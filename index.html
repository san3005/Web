How it works

SenseBridge AI is a personalized mental health assistant, leveraging all interactions to deliver real-time psychometric analysis and insights.

1

Upload a Video

Start by uploading a video of the session or interaction. AI processes the video to extract emotional cues, behavioral patterns, and other psychometric data.

2

In-Depth Analysis

The AI analyzes the video and generates a detailed report and summary on the mental condition and emotional trends observed in the patient.

3

Synchronized Playback
